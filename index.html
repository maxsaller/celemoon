RSVP

We're so grateful to all of you who have expressed excitement about joining us in Italy to celebrate our marriage. As this is a very intimate occasion, we are kindly asking for firm RSVP's at this time, so we can manage headcount and make sure to assign bedrooms accordingly. Please note that, unfortunately, we won't be able to accommodate any babies or children at the villa.

Kindly RSVP by July 30th and let us know what days you plan on joining us, as well as any additional information we may need to know.
